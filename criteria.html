Criteria for Getting an Adobe Premiere Pro Certificate

This certificate is given to participants who have successfully completed Adobe Premiere Pro training by meeting the following criteria:

  • Complete all Adobe Premiere Pro training modules, including:
    • Video editing basics
    • Use of effects and transitions
    • Color grading technique
    • Audio editing
    • Export and video format
  • Submit a final video project with a score of at least 85%.
  • Actively participate in discussions and practices during training.
